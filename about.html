✦ Our Heritage

Our Story

A journey of passion, craft, and unwavering trust

Bijay Shrestha

✦ Founder & Master Jeweller

The story of Pathivara Jewellers is, at its heart, the story of one man's extraordinary dedication. Bijay Shrestha founded Pathivara Jewellers in 2007 at a remarkably young age — not because it was the easy path, but because jewellery making was his calling.

Growing up surrounded by the rich traditions of Jhapa, Bijay was captivated by the artistry of fine jewellery — the way light dances off a well-cut stone, the perfect symmetry of a hand-hammered gold bangle, the deep cultural significance woven into every ornament worn at a wedding or festival.

With little more than a dream, immense skill, and an iron commitment to honesty, he opened his first shop at Mechinagar-13, Charaali, Jhapa. In those early years, every day was a lesson — in metallurgy, in gemstones, in customer trust, and in the quiet discipline of running a business with integrity.

"I never saw jewellery as a product to sell. I see it as a memory to create — something a family will pass down for generations." — Bijay Shrestha

Today, after more than 17 years, Pathivara Jewellers is a trusted name across Jhapa. From simple daily-wear gold rings to elaborate bridal sets, every piece still carries the personal touch of its founder — meticulous, honest, and deeply rooted in tradition.
