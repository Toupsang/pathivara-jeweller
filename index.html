✦ Our Heritage

A Legacy Born of Passion & Dedication

◆

Pathivara Jewellers was founded in 2007 by Bijay Shrestha with a simple yet profound vision — to bring master craftsmanship and genuine trust to every piece of jewellery. Starting at a remarkably young age, Bijay built the business from the ground up, guided by an unwavering passion for the art of jewellery making.

Nestled in the heart of Mechinagar-13, Charaali, Jhapa, our shop has become a beloved landmark for families seeking jewellery that carries meaning — be it a wedding ornament, a birthday gift, or a cherished heirloom.

"Every ornament we craft carries the soul of our tradition and the precision of modern artistry." — Bijay Shrestha, Founder
