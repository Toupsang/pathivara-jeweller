Panchdhatu Gold Necklace

NPR 6,55,000

A masterwork of traditional Nepali jewellery design, this Panchdhatu Gold Necklace is crafted using the ancient five-metal alloy technique — gold, silver, copper, zinc, and iron — believed to carry auspicious properties in Nepali and Hindu culture.

Each bead and pendant is shaped by hand, finished with intricate filigree detailing that reflects the highest level of craftsmanship our workshop is known for. The deep lustrous gold tone and graceful drape make it a timeless centrepiece for any ceremonial or festive occasion.

This necklace is an heirloom piece — designed not just for today, but to be cherished and passed down through generations.
