Gold Jhumka Earrings

NPR 1,99,999

The Jhumka is one of the most iconic forms of traditional South Asian jewellery, and our Gold Jhumka Earrings honour that legacy with extraordinary craftsmanship. Each earring is bell-shaped with an intricate hand-engraved exterior and a delicate fringe of tiny gold beads that sway gently with every movement.

Made entirely in 22K gold, these Jhumkas are substantial in presence yet balanced in weight — comfortable enough to wear through an entire wedding celebration. The hook clasp is designed for both security and easy wearing.

These earrings are a beloved choice for bridal wear, Teej, Dashain, and any festive occasion that calls for traditional elegance.
