22K Gold Filigree Bangles

NPR 2,95,000

Our 22K Gold Filigree Bangles represent the pinnacle of traditional Nepali metalwork. Filigree — the art of weaving fine gold wire into delicate lace-like patterns — is one of the most demanding techniques in jewellery making, and our artisans have spent years mastering it.

Each bangle in this pair features an intricate geometric pattern inspired by ancient Nepali mandala motifs. The result is a piece that is simultaneously bold and delicate — substantial in gold content, yet refined in its visual expression.

Sold as a pair, these bangles are ideal for brides, for gifting on auspicious occasions, or for collectors who appreciate rare artisan craftsmanship.
