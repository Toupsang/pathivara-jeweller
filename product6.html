Classic 22K Gold Band

NPR 28,000

Sometimes, simplicity is the most profound statement. Our Classic 22K Gold Band is the embodiment of that philosophy — a clean, perfectly proportioned ring that wears as beautifully on an ordinary Tuesday as it does on a wedding anniversary.

Crafted from solid 22K gold and polished to a smooth high-mirror finish, this band is designed for everyday wear without compromising on purity or presence. The inside of the band is comfort-fit, rounded to reduce pressure, making it ideal for extended wear.

Available in widths from 3mm to 6mm, this band can also be engraved with a name, date, or short message — a perfect personalised gift for loved ones.
